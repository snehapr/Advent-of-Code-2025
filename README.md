# Advent of Code 2025 at JPMorgan Chase

**About the Event**  
Advent of Code (AoC) is an annual global programming competition held every December, featuring a series of daily coding puzzles. At JPMorgan Chase, AoC is celebrated as a collaborative and competitive event, bringing together developers, analysts, and technology enthusiasts from across the firm. The event fosters a spirit of learning, problem-solving, and teamwork, while also encouraging friendly competition among colleagues.

**My AoC 2025 Journey**
- **Completion:** I successfully completed all 12 days of puzzles (24 stars), solving both parts of each daily challenge.
- **Duration:** The challenge was completed in 12 consecutive days, demonstrating consistency and dedication.
- **Language:** All solutions were implemented in Python, leveraging its rich ecosystem for problem-solving.
- **Collaboration:** I actively engaged with fellow participants, sharing insights and discussing approaches, which enhanced the overall experience.
- **Growth:** The event helped me sharpen my coding skills, improve my logical thinking, and learn new techniques from peers.

**Event Highlights**
- **Diverse Participation:** Colleagues from different backgrounds and experience levels joined, making it a truly inclusive event.
- **Fun and Motivation:** The festive theme and daily puzzles kept motivation high, and the internal leaderboard added a fun competitive edge.
